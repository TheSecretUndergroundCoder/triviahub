Safeguarding Policy

Effective Date: 13/12/2024

Last Updated: 13/12/2024

At Trivia Hub, we are fully committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of all users, particularly children and vulnerable individuals, when engaging with our services. This Safeguarding Policy outlines the measures we take to protect individuals in educational settings and other relevant environments.

1. Our Commitment to Safeguarding

Trivia Hub is dedicated to ensuring a safe and secure environment for all users. We adhere to the following principles to ensure safeguarding across our platform:

  • Providing clear guidelines on the responsible use of our services by educators, students, and parents.
  • Implementing strong security measures to protect personal data and user interactions.
  • Ensuring that data is processed in accordance with legal and ethical standards, including GDPR and child protection laws.

2. Safeguarding in Educational Settings

Trivia Hub recognizes that our services are often used in educational environments, and we take extra steps to safeguard the privacy and safety of students:

  • Supervised Access: We recommend that students use our platform under the guidance of educators or guardians to ensure safe and appropriate use.
  • Data Handling: All personal information collected in educational settings is managed by specialist teams who follow stringent safeguarding protocols.
  • Parental Consent: For children under the age of 13, we require verifiable parental or guardian consent before collecting or processing any personal data.

3. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

We comply with national and international laws that protect children and vulnerable individuals, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR): We ensure that data processing follows the requirements of GDPR, with special consideration for children's data.
  • Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA): For users in educational settings, we ensure compliance with FERPA guidelines regarding the privacy of student data.
  • Child Protection Laws: We comply with child protection laws that regulate how children's personal information can be collected, stored, and used.

4. Reporting Concerns

If you have any safeguarding concerns or if you suspect that someone is using Trivia Hub in a way that compromises the safety of others, please contact us immediately:

Contact Us

We take all concerns seriously and will act swiftly to address any safeguarding issues.

5. How We Protect Your Information

Trivia Hub uses industry-standard security measures to protect the personal data of all users, including:

  • Encryption: We use encryption protocols to safeguard the transfer and storage of personal data.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access sensitive data, and they are trained to follow strict privacy and safeguarding guidelines.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular audits of our systems to ensure they meet the latest safeguarding and security standards.

6. Changes to the Safeguarding Policy

We may update this Safeguarding Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the law or our operational practices. Any changes will be communicated through updates on our website.
